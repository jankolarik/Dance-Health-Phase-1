Dance Health Phase 1 with wearables and cameras:

a UCL student project in partnership with:

DanceHealth is a project that aims to create software for iPhones and AppleWatches using the depth camera and skeletal tracking with the Apple ARKit as well as Apple HealthKit data to evaluate health metrics and skill.

This technology aims to help students at Arthur Murray follow a health journey, both physical and mental, throughout the course of their lessons, as well as improve their dancing skills.
