Deployment Manual

Apple Watch App

Prerequisites: A mac with Xcode installed, an iPhone with an Apple ID Account and an Apple Watch. All devices used must be updated to the latest software.

  1. Email zcabkol@ucl.ac.uk with a message saying that you would like the code for the apple watch and I will send you the project as soon as possible.
  2. Open the project in Xcode.
  3. On the top left of Xcode, click on the navigator called ‘DanceHealthTracking’ with the blue blueprint icon on its left.
  4. In the signing & capabilities tab in the top middle part of you screen, click on the ‘automatically manage signing’ and select you ‘Team’ to your Apple ID. In case it is not there automatically, click on ‘add account’ and add your account.
  5. Connect your iPhone device to your mac via a charging cable and leave the iPhone unlocked. On the iPhone it might ask you if you ‘trust this computer’. Please press yes.
  6. On the top left corner of Xcode instead of ‘Generic watchOS device’, you should be able to select you Apple Watch device.
  7. Select your device and click the run button on the top left; the code should install in approximately one minute.
  8. Once this is completed, feel free to disconnect any devices and the Dance Health Tracking app will be ready on your Apple Watch.
  9. At first initialization, it will ask you to grant it certain permissions.
  10. After accepting these permissions, press the start button and start dancing!
