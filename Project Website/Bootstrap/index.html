About our Project

DanceHealth is a project that aims to create software for Kinect 2 Cameras and AppleWatches using the depth camera and skeletal tracking as well as Apple HealthKit data to evaluate health metrics and skill.

This technology aims to help students at Arthur Murray follow a health journey and track their improvements, throughout the course of their lessons, in order to gain the best experience possible and quantify the benefits they are gaining.
