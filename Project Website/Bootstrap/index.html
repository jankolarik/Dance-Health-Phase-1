Dance Health Phase 1: with Wearables and 3D Cameras

About our Project

DanceHealth is a student-led project that aims to create software for the Kinect 2 Camera and the Apple Watch using the depth camera and skeletal tracking as well as Apple HealthKit data to evaluate health metrics and skill.

This technology aims to help students at Arthur Murray follow a health journey and track their improvements, throughout the course of their lessons, in order to gain the best experience possible and quantify the benefits they are gaining.
